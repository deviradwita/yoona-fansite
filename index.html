Im Yoon-ah

Member of GirlGeneration

(Korean: 임윤아; born May 30, 1990),

Yoona is a South Korean singer and actress. After training for five years, she debuted as a member of girl group Girls' Generation (and later its subgroup Girls' Generation-Oh!GG) in August 2007, which went on to become one of the best-selling artists in South Korea and one of South Korea's most popular girl groups worldwide.

Yoona's Skills

Acting

Yoona has participated in various television dramas, notably You Are My Destiny (2008), which marked her career breakthrough and earned her the Best New Actress awards at the 23rd KBS Drama Awards and the 45th Baeksang Arts Awards.

Yoona has since achieved further public attention and acting acclaim with a variety of role-types in:

  • Love Rain (2012)
  • Prime Minister & I (2013)
  • The K2 (2016)
  • The King in Love (2017)
  • Hush (2020–21)
  • Big Mouth (2022)
  • Confidential Assignment (2017)
  • Exit (2019)
  • Confidential Assignment 2 (2022)

Singing and Dancing

Yoona was a trainee under S.M.Entertainment for around five to six years. She made her official debut as a member of Girls' Generation in August 2007, becoming the "center" of the group.

She is a lead dancer, supporting rapper, supporting vocalist, and the visualist in K-pop girl group, Girls' Generation, as well as the youngest member and leader of Girls' Generation second subunit Oh!GG.
